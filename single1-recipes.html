Chocolate Pear Spice Cake

Step 1 Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 10 inch springform pan. Step 2 Use an electric mixer to beat the eggs, applesauce, molasses, and butter in a large bowl. Beat in the cake mix along with the cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and orange peel. Mix on medium speed for 4 minutes. Pour batter into prepared pan. Top the unbaked cake with pear slices; sprinkle evenly with the pecans and chopped chocolate. Step 3 Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 55 to 60 minutes. Cool for 25 minutes before removing from pan.

prep time

20 min

cook time

1 hour

ingredients

eggs

3 eggs

3 tablespoons molasses

½ cup butter, melted

2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

1 teaspoon ground nutmeg

¼ teaspoon ground cloves

1 tablespoon finely shredded orange peel

1 small Bosc pear, peeled and thinly sliced

1 (2.6 ounce) bar milk chocolate, coarsely chopped

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
